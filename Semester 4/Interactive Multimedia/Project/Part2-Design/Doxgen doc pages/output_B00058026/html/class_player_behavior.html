void PlayerBehavior.LostLife ( ) [private]

actions for when player loses a life:

  1. reduce player's lives left by 1
  2. reset health back to 100%
  3. reset oxygen back to 100%
  4. play lost life sound

void PlayerBehavior.Update ( ) [private]

actions for every frame:

  1. reduce health by 0.1
  2. reduce oxygen by 0.1
  3. ensure health never goes above 100
  4. ensure oxygen never goes above 100
  5. see if player should lose a life due to health going too low
  6. see if player should lose a life due to oxygen levels going too low
